Classic Splendor asset pack
===========================

Source: /Users/tryanks/WebstormProjects/splendor/client/src/types.ts

That local reference project resolves classic card, noble, token, and deck-back images from:
https://raw.githubusercontent.com/hexanome-04/splendor/master/client/public/images

Imported here for the classic multiplayer Splendor variant:
- 90 development cards merged into cards-tier1.webp, cards-tier2.webp, and cards-tier3.webp atlases.
- 10 nobles merged into nobles.webp.
- 6 WebP-optimized gem tokens in tokens/
- 3 deck backs in card-backs/

Generated locally:
- tabletops/jewel-felt.webp: AI-generated dark walnut tabletop texture with subtle brass filigree and gem inlay for the classic Splendor table, downscaled and WebP-optimized.
- card-backs/noble.webp: AI-generated square noble deck back, style-guided by the three imported Classic tier card backs and WebP-optimized.
